Producer's Note: A Busy Week

Submitted by Bonnie on Thu, 02/17/2011 - 08:48

Well, this has been a crazy week for us at STT. Monday's launch went so well. Thanks to everyone who watched, and who continues to drop by. We have over 2000 visits to the web site, and have viewers from 25 countries including Canada, the US, the UK, Germany, Australia, Sweden, South Africa, France, Brazil, Argentina, the Czech Republic, Israel and even South Korea! Please spread the word ... we aren't big in Japan yet! :-)

We have been organizing like crazy this week: putting the final touches on episode 2, and planning a shoot for this weekend. We will be shooting three more episodes - you'll see them in late-March/early April. Oh, and in addition, there were media interviews, and sending out press releases to attract more attention.

We are thrilled that so many of you are enjoying the show. Drop by Monday when Bob goes ballistic about what the Tarts did in the gym, and Sue makes her famous Lavender Shortbread (recipe included!).

Have a great weekend, everyone! We'll be busy "on set", filming more of the Tarts' hilarious adventures.

-Bonnie
