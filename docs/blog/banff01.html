STT Finalist at Banff World Media Festival

Submitted by Bonnie on Wed, 05/25/2011 - 15:30

Sweet Tarts Takeaway has been selected as one of 6 finalists in the Entertainment category of the Pilots Competition at the 2011 Banff World Media Festival (BWMF). BWMF is the world's largest global development and production marketplace, and has been attracting international producers of television/media content to beautiful Banff since 1979.

It is a huge achievement just to be a finalist at this prestigious festival, and we are overwhelmed and delighted just to make it this far, and in such exceptional company as our co-finalists. We are scrambling now to get everything together for the festival, which runs from 12-15 June. Our wonderful cast and crew have done an exceptional job pulling the show together, and it is great to see that work recognized in this way.

Bonnie
