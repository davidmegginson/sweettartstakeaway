Tyler Kealey, Musician

Tyler Kealey is a songwriter who sings with passion and conviction to match his emotionally-driven lyrics. He is constantly lending his talent to local Ottawa bands, and has played with Silver Creek, the The Cooper Brothers, Charlie Major, and Roxanne Potvin. Tyler plays a successful solo pop act as well and released his first full length album, Characters, in 2009. Two songs from the album, Only Love and The Whiz Kid provide background music in Episode 3.
Visit him at https://www.tylerkealey.com/fr_home.cfm.
